RIO = Rivers Influencing the Ocean

Sep 17, 2021
Video Follow Follow

Assessing the glacier projection uncertainties in the Southern Andes (40-56°S) from a catchment perspective

The RIO (river in English) project seeks to improve understanding of the environment and encourage reasoned decision-making with critical and reflective thinking. Chilean society, although it values rivers and coastal systems, still has a strong lack of knowledge about the virtuous connections that are generated between them.
